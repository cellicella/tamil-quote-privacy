Privacy Policy for Tamil Quotes App

Last Updated: December 2023

Introduction

Welcome to Tamil Quotes ("we," "our," or "us"). We built the Tamil Quotes app as a free application. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Tamil Quotes app is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to assure you that:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not collect any usage data
  • We do not collect device information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools

Local Data Storage

The app stores your favorite quotes locally on your device using Android's built-in storage system. This data:

  • Remains only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible by us or any third parties
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you. The app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

Share Feature

When you use the "Share" feature to share quotes with others, the sharing is handled by your device's native sharing capabilities. We do not have access to or control over what you share or with whom you share it.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to our privacy policy.
